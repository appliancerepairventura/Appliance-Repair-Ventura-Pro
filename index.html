Why Ventura Homes Experience More Appliance Wear

Living near the Pacific Ocean has many advantages, but it also creates unique challenges for household appliances. Salt carried by ocean breezes can accelerate corrosion on electrical components, while humidity affects control boards and wiring. Hard water leaves mineral deposits inside dishwashers, washing machines, and ice makers. Our technicians routinely repair these issues and recommend maintenance that helps extend appliance life.
